## 🔄 Major Changes Implemented

### 1. App Identity Transformation

- **App Name**: "Farm Fresh" → "Coffee Pro"
- **Theme Colors**: Green farm theme → Coffee brown/chocolate theme
- **Branding**: Complete visual identity overhaul
- **Icons**: Agriculture → Coffee-specific iconography

### 2. Core System Architecture

#### New Models Created

- Season: Yearly coffee collection periods (2024/2025 format)
- CoffeeCollection: Replaces coffee delivery with coffee-specific fields
- UnitOfMeasure: Flexible measurement units (kg, liters, pieces)
- ProductCategory: Product organization system
- Product: Inventory items with pricing and specifications
- Stock: Inventory level tracking with movements
- Sale: Sales transactions with cash/credit support
- SaleItem: Individual sale line items
- Repayment: Credit payment tracking

#### New Services Developed

- SeasonService: Complete season lifecycle management
- CoffeeCollectionService: Coffee collection processing
- InventoryService: Comprehensive inventory and sales management

#### New Controllers Added

- SeasonController: Season management UI logic
- CoffeeCollectionController: Collection form handling
- InventoryController: Inventory operations and state

### 3. Database Schema Evolution

#### New Tables Created

- seasons: Coffee collection periods
- coffee_collections: Collection records
- units_of_measure: Measurement units
- product_categories: Product organization
- products: Inventory catalog
- stock: Inventory levels
- sales: Sales transactions
- sale_items: Sale line items
- repayments: Credit payments

#### Updated Tables

- system_settings: Added coffee-specific fields
- organization_settings: Updated for coffee society branding
